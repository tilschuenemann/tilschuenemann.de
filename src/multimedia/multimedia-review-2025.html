multimedia review 2025

In this post I want to review all the media I've consumed in 2025:

  • movies
  • shows
  • music
  • games
  • books
My motives for this post are three-fold:

As a first, I want to preserve some of my taste and experience. I have accounts for last.fm and Letterboxd, where my music and movie history gets logged. Maybe I can consistently create a post every year, and look back at them, and see how I changed.

My second motive is that I want to inspire you to watch a movie or listen to an album that you didn't even know before you read this. Some items here are my personal gems, but I can't express their worth in a post like this, although I would love to, but I can't (at least yet).

movies

2025 feels like it has been the year with the fewest movies watched. It's hard to cram them into an evening when I'm blasted from work. Also, my and my SOs taste differ a lot. Anyway, here are movies that retrospectively stood out to me. I remember watching

  • Series 7: The Contenders (2001)
  • Grey Gardens (1975)
  • Manhattan (1979)
  • Lady Snowblood (1973)
  • Close-Up (1990)
  • Where Is The Friends House? (1987)
  • Through The Olive Trees (1994)
  • The Gleaners and I (2000)
  • Bad Boy Bubby (1993)
  • The French Connection (1971)
  • La Notte (1961)
  • Come and See (1985)
  • As Good As It Gets (1997)
As Good As It Gets wasn't exactly good but I think it warrants a good base for the discussion of neuroticism.

  • Artificial Brain - Labyrinth Constellation
  • Atheist - Unquestionable Presence
  • Gigan - Undulating Waves Of Rainbiotic Iridescence
  • Anata - Under A Stone With No Inscription
  • Pyrrhon - Exhaust
  • Imperial Triumphant - Vile Luxury
