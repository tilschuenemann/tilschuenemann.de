Bacon Onion Jam

Ingredients

  • diced bacon
  • maple syrup
  • balsamic vinegar
  • white onion
  • brown sugar
  • salt
  • pepper
  • thyme
  • smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp red chili flakes
  • parsley

Instructions

  • Add bacon to a pot, put the heat on medium-high and render the fat, until bacon is crunchy but not burnt.
  • Dice onions. Remove bacon from the pot while keeping the bacon fat, and add the diced onions. Turn heat down to medium.
  • The onions should start to golden. Caramallize them if you want and have the time.
  • Add chili, salt, pepper, brown sugar, garlic, parsley, smoked paprika and the bacon back into the pot.
  • Add balsamic vinegar and maple syrup and balance the sweetness / acidity based on your liking.
  • Reduce this mixture as long as you like.
