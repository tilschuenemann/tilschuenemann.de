Pineapple Hot Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 pineapple
  • 2-3 chili peppers
  • 4-5 cloves of garlic
  • 1 lime
  • ginger
  • apple cider vinegar
  • salt
  • sugar
  • cumin
  • fresh coriander leaves

Instructions

  • Remove outer layer from pineapple.
  • Optional: Get a good sear on pineapple slices.
  • Blend all ingredients and put into a container.
