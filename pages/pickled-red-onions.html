Pickled Red Onions

Ingredients

  • red onions
  • 5% vinegar
  • salt
  • sugar
  • pepper
  • bay leaves
  • garlic
  • mustard seeds

Instructions

  • Put water and vinegar with a 1:1 ratio in a pot, bring to a boil and dissolve sugar. Let it cool after that.
  • Cut the red onions into rings or half moons and put them into a heat-resistant container.
  • Add bay leaf, garlic into container.
  • Fill the container with the brine until all onions are covered.
  • Store in the fridge.
